Target Audience

This website will target all entrepreneurs and small-business owners in the city of Anthem. Anyone interested in getting into one of these spaces would also be interested in joining the chamber of commerce.

Personas

Jack

Jack is a 28 year old entrepreneur from Phoenix, Arizona. He has recently graduated from ASU business college and is ready to explore business opprotunities in Anthem. Jack currently is working solo but would love to expand and find team members with similar goals to his. Outside of work, Jack loves to spend time with his friends and family. He also loves to be involved in his community in any way. Jack is excited for his new life in Anthem and cannot wait to become a member of the chamber of commerce to advance in his goals as a successful entrepreneur.

Jessica

Jessica has been a member of the anthem community for five years now. She loves this small city and has intentions to live here for many years to come. Jessica is 32 and a mother of 2. Jessica is also a college graduate who has recently started a small business making and selling candles. She started this business 2 years ago and has finally found a few business partners who also live in the area to help her grow her brand. Jessica hopes to expand in other places but doesn't know how to get started. Jessica hope to gain more confidence as a small-business owner by joining the Anthem Chamber of Commerce.
