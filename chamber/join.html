Non-Profit
  • Forge connections with organizations that share your values and magnify your communal influence.
  • Increase your visibility at Anthem Chamber events and through our promotional outlets.
  • Seize valuable opportunities to network and forge key partnerships.
  • Collaborate with other non-profits in Anthem to co-create impactful community projects.
  • Highlight your non-profit's goals and activities with special placements in our chamber publications and newsletters.
  • Access exclusive training and workshops designed for non-profits.
Bronze ($100 month)
  • Benefit from all Non-Profit membership perks, with added advantages:
  • Feature in our online business directory to boost your digital footprint.
  • Get special invites to educational seminars and workshops for business enhancement.
  • Leverage our social media platforms to promote your business and expand your audience.
  • Network at monthly events and connect with local entrepreneurs and professionals.
  • Enjoy reduced rates for ad placements in our marketing materials, broadening your reach.
Silver ($200 month)
  • Reap all Bronze membership rewards, with additional privileges:
  • Elevate your business's profile with priority listing in our directory and website.
  • Contribute to influential chamber committees and shape local policies.
  • Share your knowledge as a guest speaker at events, establishing your industry leadership.
  • Get first dibs on sponsoring chamber events and programs, enhancing your brand's prestige.
  • Partake in members-only business development workshops and seminars to sharpen your acumen.
Gold ($300 month)
  • Enjoy the pinnacle of membership, combining all benefits from other tiers, plus:
  • Stand out as a key supporter of the Chamber at flagship events.
  • Receive VIP invites to exclusive networking events and business showcases.
  • Benefit from prominent logo display on the Chamber's website and event materials for maximum exposure.
  • Access high-level networking opportunities with key community figures and leaders.
  • Be featured in a dedicated section of our newsletter, spotlighting your business's successes.
